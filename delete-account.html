How to Delete Your Account

You can delete your Stepory account at any time. Once deleted, your data cannot be recovered.

What gets deleted

When you delete your account, we permanently remove:

  • Your profile and account settings
  • Your step history
  • Your challenges and achievements
  • Your friends list and group memberships
  • Your profile photo and other stored data

How to delete your account (in the app)

  1. Open the Stepory app on your Android device.
  2. Tap the menu icon (☰) to open the navigation drawer.
  3. Tap Profile.
  4. Scroll down to the Account & Data section.
  5. Tap Delete Account (the red button with a delete icon).
  6. Read the confirmation message and what will be deleted.
  7. If you signed in with email: Enter your password to confirm, then tap Delete.
  8. If you signed in with Google: Follow the on-screen prompts to confirm deletion.

This action cannot be undone. Make sure you have exported any data you want to keep (use Export Data in Profile before deleting) and that you really want to delete your account.
